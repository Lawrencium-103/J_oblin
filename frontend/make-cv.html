Tips for a Great CV
  • Include numbers and metrics — "reduced cost by 20%", "managed 50+ clients"
  • List all software, tools, and languages you know
  • Mention achievements, not just duties
  • Include volunteer work, internships, and side projects
  • For Nigeria: include NYSC status, local certifications
  • For International: highlight remote collaboration, timezone flexibility
  • Be honest — never fabricate experience
