API Setup

Get Your Free API Key

Joblin works with a free built-in model, but you can get faster and better results by adding your own API key from NVIDIA or Groq. Both are free to sign up and give you credits to start immediately. No credit card required.

N

NVIDIA (build.nvidia.com)

NVIDIA offers a free API tier with generous credits for new users. Their Nemotron and Llama models are great for CV tailoring.

  • Step 1: Go to build.nvidia.com and click "Sign Up" in the top right corner.
  • Step 2: Sign up with your Google account, Microsoft account, or any email address. Fill in your name and create a password.
  • Step 3: Check your email for a confirmation link. Click it to verify your account.
  • Step 4: Once logged in, click your profile picture or initials in the top right, then select "Personal Settings" or "API Keys" from the menu.
  • Step 5: Click "Generate New Key" or "Create API Key". Give it a name like "joblin-app" so you can remember it.
  • Step 6: Copy the key that appears (it starts with "nvapi-"). It looks something like nvapi-xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx.
  • Step 7: Paste this key into Joblin's Settings page under "NVIDIA API Key" and click Save.
Tip: NVIDIA gives you free credits when you sign up. You can check your remaining credits in your dashboard at build.nvidia.com. If you run out, just generate a new key or switch to the free built-in model in Settings.
G

Groq (console.groq.com)

Groq provides ultra-fast inference with their own LPU hardware. Their free tier includes the Llama and Mixtral models and resets daily.

  • Step 1: Go to console.groq.com and click "Sign Up" or "Get Started".
  • Step 2: You can sign up with your Google account or GitHub account for the quickest setup. Alternatively, use your email address.
  • Step 3: Verify your email if you signed up with email. Google/GitHub sign-ups are instant.
  • Step 4: After logging in, look for "API Keys" in the left sidebar menu. Click it.
  • Step 5: Click "Create API Key". Give it a name like "joblin-app".
  • Step 6: Copy the key that appears (it starts with "gsk_"). It looks something like gsk_xxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx.
  • Step 7: Paste this key into Joblin's Settings page under "Groq API Key" and click Save.
Tip: Groq's free tier gives you a daily limit (usually thousands of requests). Check your usage at console.groq.com under "Rate Limits". The free tier resets every 24 hours, so you never need to pay.
S

Where to Enter Your Key in Joblin

Once you have your API key from NVIDIA or Groq:

  • Log into Joblin and click Settings in the sidebar.
  • Scroll to the API Keys section.
  • Paste your NVIDIA key or Groq key into the corresponding field.
  • Click Save. Joblin will test the key to make sure it works.
  • You can also toggle between the built-in free model and your own key at any time.
Heads up: Your API keys are encrypted and stored securely. Joblin never shares them. You can remove them anytime from the same Settings page.
Complete Guide

How to Use Joblin

Joblin is built for Nigerian job seekers who want to stop applying blind and start targeting every application. Here is exactly how it works, from account creation to walking into your interview.

1

Create Your Account

Sign up at joblin.app/register with your name, email, and password. No credit card needed. No trial period. The free AI model is included from day one.

  • Your data stays yours, encrypted and private
  • You can toggle between the free default AI and your own API keys in Settings
  • Once registered, you land on the Dashboard, your command centre
2

Build Your CV Profile

Joblin needs to know who you are before it can match you. You have three ways to build your CV:

  • Make My CV (Recommended): Paste your raw career text — previous roles, achievements, education, projects. Joblin's AI extracts everything into a structured CV. You can speak it using the voice narration feature if you prefer.
  • CV Editor: Use the structured form to fill in each section manually. Great for fine-tuning specific details.
  • Plain Text Import: Paste any existing CV as raw text and the AI parses it into sections automatically.

Tip: Include specific numbers in your achievements (percentages, amounts, volumes) — the AI uses these to build stronger bullets when tailoring.

3

Add Your Target Jobs

Tell Joblin what you are looking for. There are four ways to find jobs:

  • Browse Jobs: Joblin scrapes 40+ job boards (LinkedIn, Indeed, MyJobMag, Hot Nigerian Jobs, UN, WHO, Web3, Greenhouse, Ashby, and more). Filter by category, source, graduate level, or search by keyword.
  • Manual Entry: Paste a job URL or manually type the title, company, and description of any role you found elsewhere.
  • Category Browsing: Filter by sector — Tech, NGO/Development, Oil & Gas, Government, Finance, Health, Web3/Crypto, International Remote.
  • Match Scores: Every job shows a match score (0-100) based on keyword overlap with your CV profile — so you know which ones to prioritize.
4

Tailor Your CV & Cover Letter

This is where Joblin does what no template can. Click Tailor My CV on any job — the AI rewrites your entire CV for that specific role:

  • Professional Summary — rewritten to mirror the job description and lead with your most relevant achievement
  • Core Competencies — regrouped and reworded to match the skills the employer is asking for
  • Experience Bullets — every achievement bullet is rewritten to show relevance to the target role. You keep the same title and company; the language shifts to demonstrate transferable skills
  • Cover Letter — a full 4-5 paragraph letter written specifically for this job, not a fill-in-the-blank template. It references the company, ties your past work to their needs, and avoids every banned AI cliche
  • HR Email — optional short pitch email you can send directly to the hiring manager

Note: Joblin never fabricates experience. It reframes what you have done to show why it matters for the role you are applying for.

5

Download & Apply

Once tailored, download your documents:

  • CV — available as DOCX and PDF. Professionally formatted with clean single-column ATS-friendly layout. Multiple design templates applied randomly so every generation looks different.
  • Cover Letter — also in DOCX and PDF. Proper letter format with your contact header, date, salutation, body paragraphs, and sign-off.
  • HR Email — copy the generated email or use it as a draft in your mail client.

Pro tip: Always review the tailored CV before sending. Read every bullet. If something does not sound like you, edit it in the CV Editor and regenerate. The AI gives you a starting point — your judgment makes it final.

6

Repeat & Improve

Each time you tailor for a different job, Joblin saves a new version. Your original CV stays untouched — only the tailored copy changes.

  • Tailor as many jobs as you want — no limits
  • Update your CV profile anytime and regenerations reflect the latest version
  • The match scores help you focus on jobs where you have the strongest alignment
  • Use the quality scores on each tailored CV to check: are there enough metrics? Is the cover letter strong enough?

Reminder: Joblin updates job listings every 15 minutes from 40+ sources. Check back regularly for new matches.
